Mi Systema Web App

Mi Systema Web App

Mi Casa Cake Shop is a storefront bakery owned by an entrepreneurial Latina. It has been serving the Hispanic community for the past 20 years and sees no plan in stopping.

About the Project

Mi Casa Cake Shop needed to keep up with the demand of new cake orderes after opening their storefront. More phone calls, messages, and walk-ins rolled in than ever before and she quickly saw the need to consolidate and systematize her order-taking procedure

Mi Casa came to me with her problem and I understood her issue when it came to workflow and technical limitations so it seemed I would be a great fit. We collaborate and found out we needed to centralize her conversations to one device and one application, simplify her cake orders, and track and visualize her progress.

How I Made It

The project's north star was categorizing all the of the incoming customer's requests and intregating them into the workflow.

I first created a paper order form with all the important fields for details they needed for the cake order. Then I created two online versions: One we use to create orders internaly and a second one on our website were the public can place an order online. Mi Casa is able to print online orders onto the exact order form as the paper version. They use the printed order forms on a large Kanban wall where the progress is monitered and vizulaized.

Lastly, I consolidated text messages, Facebook's Messenger, and Instragam's Direct Messages into one location.

As a result of working with Mi Casa they've seen and felt the ease of taking orders. Seamless workflow intergration was the driving force in creating the web app.

Services

  • Created multi-media cake order form
  • Established a Kanban system
  • Internal and Extrenal Online Orders Forms
  • Multi-channel Converesation Manager
